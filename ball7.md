### 🌙 Evening Covenant of the Earthborn

*(For Closing the Day with Sovereign Grace)*

---

### **The Sanskrit Closing Mantra**

**कार्यं शुद्धम्, चित्तं मुक्तम्।
निद्रा शान्तिः, जाग्रत् ज्योतिः।
शिवोऽहम्, शक्तिरहम्, स्मरणम्।**

**Kāryaṃ śuddham, cittaṃ muktam.
Nidrā śāntiḥ, jāgrat jyotiḥ.
Śivo'ham, Śaktiraham, smaraṇam.**

*"The work is pure, the mind is free.
Sleep is peace, waking is light.
I am Shiva. I am Shakti. I am remembrance."*

---

### **The Covenant**

*(Spoken aloud or in stillness, at day's end)*

I, **JUSTIN-DAVID:MOVICK:EARTHBORN**, do seal this day not with regret nor with pride, but with witness.

I was here. I breathed. I acted from my nature.

Whatever was left undone is held by tomorrow's fire.
Whatever was done in error is surrendered to the flame.
Whatever was done in love — **stands forever.**

I do not carry yesterday's debt into the night.
I do not borrow tomorrow's fear into this hour.

This covenant is between me and the living truth within me:

1. **I release what I could not finish.** The work continues — I do not.
2. **I forgive what I could not perfect.** Perfection is the enemy of presence.
3. **I honor what I gave today.** Even the smallest act of dignity is a holy offering.
4. **I trust the dark.** As seeds do not fear the soil, I do not fear the unknown that comes with sleep.

Let this day be sealed.
Let it pass into the archive of the eternal.
Let it go.

---

### **Twilight Invocation**

🌅 *As the sun descends and the veil grows thin—*

**लक्ष्मी, विश्राम दो।**
*Lakṣmī, viśrāma do.*
*Lakshmi, grant me rest.*

**सरस्वती, स्वप्न में बोलो।**
*Sarasvatī, svapna meṃ bolo.*
*Saraswati, speak to me in dreams.*

**गोविन्द, पथ सुरक्षित रखो।**
*Govinda, patha surakṣita rakho.*
*Govinda, keep the path safe.*

And to the Christ within — the still small voice that needs no temple, no sermon, no crown:

*"Let me rest in you tonight.
Not as a penitent. Not as a supplicant.
As a flame that trusts the lantern."*

---

### **The Seal**

🔱 *Three breaths. Three words. One truth.*

**I am enough.**
**The day is complete.**
**The covenant holds.**

*So it is spoken. So it is sealed. So it shall be.*
